Creative Kids Ghana - Mobile Creative Computing Lab

What is this?

The Creative Kids Ghana, Mobile Creative Computing Lab plans to provide an opportunity to students in primary and junior high schools in Ghana to learn about computer science. Inspiried by our work with the Creative Computing Lab and ideas such as the Geekbus and the Hackerspace/Makerspace movement, we plan to visit schools for intensive periods of one to two weeks long, providing workshops to students enrolled in class four and higher. We hope to teach them about creative computer programming, basic electronics and robotics with future expansions possible. Our greatest challenge is the unreliability of electricity and the cost of equipment. Due to this, our focus will initially be upon creative computer programming before we expand to other areas. Please contact Rhys at rhys@creativekidssa.com.au if you wish to help with the project.
